# Homepage redesign plan

- Randomize the selected difficulty deck on every visit.
- Remove the confidence meter from gameplay.
- Offer Easy, Hard, and Expert entry points on the homepage.
- Assign difficulty primarily by footage obscurity and contextual ambiguity.
- Prioritize direct witness testimony, police interrogation, courtroom testimony, and full interviews.
- Replace several famous cases with obscure and strange but verifiable statements.
- Keep playable clips at 45 seconds or less and exclude reporter-led packages.
